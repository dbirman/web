Research Projects

Att_Awe

In the inattentional blindness literature there are lots of strange interactions between features, e.g. natural scenes are always perceived while low level features are not. Why? Here we test how feature-based attention to contrast and motion coherence alter the perceptions of these features. We model this behavior in the frame of signal detection theory and then use BOLD responses to try to understand the computations the brain uses to represent and compute this behavior.
